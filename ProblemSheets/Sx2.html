Problem 2.2.

Prove Proposition 1.2.6. (This is merely a reformulation of the definition of arbitrage; don’t be afraid!)

Solution We first verify that the first of the two conditions is equivalent to the definition. Since V0⁢(x,ϕ)=V^0⁢(x,ϕ)V_{0}(x,\phi)=\hat{V}_{0}(x,\phi), the first condition holds by definition.
