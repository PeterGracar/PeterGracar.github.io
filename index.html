About

Research and teaching in probability

Hi, I am Peter Gracar, Lecturer in Probability in the Statistics Department of the School of Mathematics at the University of Leeds.

My research focuses on random geometric graphs, particle systems, and broader percolation theory. You can find preprints, publications, and collaborators on the research page.
