Directions

Office 9.10p1 in the EC Stoner Building

Use the route options below to reach the Maths Research Deck and office 9.10p1.

Route A: Red route via Staircase 1

  1. Follow the red route on level 10. The School of Mathematics entrance is on level 9.
  2. Take a left into the E.C. Stoner building and walk past the IT Service Desk.
  3. Take the stairs on the right (Staircase 1) and go down one floor.
  4. At the bottom of the stairs, turn right and go through the door marked Maths Research Deck.

Route B: Opposite-door route

  1. Go through the IT Services door opposite the School of Mathematics.
  2. Turn right and follow the signs for Maths Research Deck.

Route C: Entrance 1 South (direct stair route)

  1. Enter through Entrance 1 South next to the bike shelter.
  2. Take the stairs up three floors, then go through the door marked Maths Research Deck.
